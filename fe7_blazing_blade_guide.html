Ninian leaves permanently around Ch. 28 (story event) — use her dancer utility while you can. Nino has the highest magic growth in the game but joins at level 5 in Ch. 24; she needs a Guiding Ring and several chapters to shine. Harken vs. Karel are mutually exclusive — see Ch. 25 notes for conditions. Hector mode adds Farina (20,000G), Karla (complex conditions), and Oswin — none are worth the cost except Oswin.
